# 🚀 QR.NEXUS

**QR.NEXUS** is a futuristic, all-in-one QR Code Studio designed for creating, customizing, securing, decoding, and exporting QR codes with a modern holographic user interface.

Unlike traditional QR generators, QR.NEXUS provides advanced customization, security features, batch processing, NFC utilities, and export tools in a single platform.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🔳 QR Generation

* Text QR Codes
* URL QR Codes
* Wi-Fi QR Codes
* Email QR Codes
* SMS QR Codes
* vCard Contact QR Codes

### 🎨 Customization

* Multiple QR Dot Styles (Square, Rounded, Circle)
* Custom Colors
* Gradient QR Codes
* Quick Theme Presets
* Adjustable QR Size
* Error Correction Levels
* Custom Frames & Labels

### 🖼 Branding

* Logo Embedding
* Custom QR Appearance
* Download as PNG
* Download as SVG

### 🔒 Security

* Password-Protected QR Codes
* Encrypted Content Support

### 📡 NFC Utilities

* NFC NDEF String Generator
* NFC-Compatible Content Preparation

### 📂 Batch Processing

* Generate Multiple QR Codes at Once
* ZIP Export Support

### 🔍 QR Decoder

* Upload QR Images
* Decode Existing QR Codes
* Copy or Reuse Decoded Content

### 📊 Analysis Tools

* QR Density Analysis
* Contrast Checker
* Scanability Recommendations

### 🖼 Export Tools

* Phone Wallpapers
* Desktop Wallpapers
* Tablet Wallpapers
* Custom QR Sharing Assets
